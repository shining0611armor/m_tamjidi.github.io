📝 Short Biography

Mohammad Javad Ahmadi was born in 1996 in Sari near the Caspian Sea, Iran. He graduated from NODET in 2015 with a 4/4 diploma, earned his B.Sc. from Amirkabir University in 2019 (GPA 3.7/4), and completed his M.Sc. at KNTU in 2022 (GPA 4/4). He’s currently a Ph.D. candidate under Prof. Hamid D. Taghirad (GPA 4/4), CTO at MediversAI & SmarTeeth, and leads the ARAS AI, VR & Software Group. His main research interests include Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Computer Vision.
