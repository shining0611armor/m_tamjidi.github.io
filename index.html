📝 Short Biography

Mehran Tamjidi was born in 1999 in Ahwaz, Iran, near the Karun River. He graduated from Dr. Hesabi Leading Government High School in Ahwaz and earned his B.Sc. in 2019 from Shahid Chamran University of Ahwaz with a GPA of 17.70/20, ranking second in his program. He completed his M.Sc. at K. N. Toosi University of Technology (KNTU) in 2022 with a GPA of 18.43/20 (equivalent to 3.9/4), ranking first in his program. He is currently conducting research in multi-modal AI, generative models, 3D vision, and domain generalization, with a focus on developing methods suitable for publication in top-tier computer vision venues.
